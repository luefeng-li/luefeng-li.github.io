Self Introduction

Yuefeng Li received the B.E. degree in mechanical engineering from the Harbin University of Science and Technology., weihai, China, in 2016 and 2020; He was a Research assistant in Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen. (2020-2021). His main research field includes robotics, Artificial muscles and Information processing.

The Chinese University of Hong Kong，Shenzhen (CUHKSZ) Shenzhen, China RA in Shenzhen Institute of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics for Society Jul.2020-Presen

  • Investigation and Analysis about twisted and coiled artificial muscle, and Writing review;
  • Made nylon 66 muscles and considered how to apply them to soft robots.

RESEARCH EXPERIENCE

The Design of a Fluidic-Muscle and Fuzzy-PID based Bionic Robot Manipulator Project Leader （Undergraduate Innovation Project, 黑龙江省大学生创新省级项目） 05/ 2019-Present

  • Employed the FluidicMuscle artificial muscles for the manipulator’s joints control;
  • Designed the pneumatic circuit for high-precision manipulator’s movement control;
  • Combined fuzzy-PID controllers with a closed loop; Designed 3D-Printed the robot structural model.

The Design of a Novel Squeezed Liquid Composite Armature Electromagnetic Railgun Project Co-Leader 05/2018-05/2019

  • Employed a low-cost liquid composite armature design to reduce the switching probability;
  • Optimized the traditional parallel fixed rail with a squeeze rail to avoid the contact failure;
  • Designed and manufactured the model of composite armature, splint and barrel.

The Design of an Automatic Potato Harvester Project Co-Leader（Undergraduate Innovation Project, 黑龙江省大学生创新省级项目）05/2017-11/2018

  • Proposed the driving, the excavating-separating systems and combined them with lifting system;
  • Completed the 3D modellings with Proe and 2D Drawings with AutoCAD;
  • Cooperated with international members and Negotiated with the sub-contractors and logistics.
